About us

The idea of Arboreal grew in 2016 when we, a devoted team of passionated arboralist, gardeners and florists got together, discussing the value of indoor airpurizers. How the many benefits of having a green indoor enviroment could encrease the healt as well as the productivity of workers in a office.

In August 2017 Arboreal opened the doors to the cosy office at Birger Jarlsgatan 12 in the very heart of Stockholm.

Today we are a team of 20 Visual desigers, florists, arborealist, gardeners, merchial visioneries and marketing team.

We also have a specialist working solely on health by indoor improvements, visual aspects, airpurity and what benefits we can achieve by just adding plants into our lives.

So let us show you all of those things which could keep you and your coworkes in a breeze of calmness.



On a mission

Arboreal revolutionizes the way you think about plants in an office environment. Arboreal isn't just a a delivery service for plants, it's a delivery service for new ideas. Arboreal makes it easy to keep your workplace fresh and oxygenated.
